Canoeing

Sail down the river with our canoeing adventure! Experience the Buffalo’s beauty firsthand as we guide you through the outdoors.

March – June is, typically, float trip season on the upper Buffalo National River. Check out the current Buffalo River Levels for paddling conditions.

At Buffalo River Adventures, we offer shuttle services to the following put-in/take-outs on the river; Steel Creek, Kyles, Pruitt, Ozark, Erbie, Hasty, and Hwy 123 (Carver). The approximate float distance and times are calculated below as well.

Lodging

Bear Ridge offers two guests a cozy, fully furnished cabin for a romantic getaway in upper Buffalo River country. The cabin has a main floor and a sleeping loft with a queen-size bed. We are pleased to offer complimentary WIFI with this cabin rental.

The cabin's kitchen features a dining bar and is fully furnished with appliances, basic meal prep utensils and cookware, a coffee maker, and even a dishwasher. In the cabin's bathroom, you'll find a lovely stone walk-in shower, along with towels, soap, and shampoo

  • Hot tub and stone firepit on patio
  • Private creekside sitting area (Coming soon!)
  • Wifi (no cell service in the area)
  • 42” TV with satellite/local channels, BluRay player, movies
  • Fully-equipped kitchen – microwave, dishwasher, coffee maker, toaster
  • Dishes, pots and pans, cleaning supplies
  • All bed and bath linens
  • Front and back porches with seating
  • Outdoor gas grill
  • Mini-split HVAC plus electric “fireplace” heater
  • Parking in front of cabin
  • Firewood for outdoor fire pit
  • Convenient to the Buffalo Outdoor Center store and the Ponca Elk Education Center
  • Just a short drive to the Ponca Access and the Lost Valley Trailhead
