Float or Not to Float?

River Level Well, Let's Check The Charts!
1.2 or less Too low to float.
1.3 - 1.7 Floatable for a short canoe trip but expect some dragging.
1.8 - 1.9 Too low for rafts, but good for low water canoeing.
2.0 - 2.4 Mild white waters and may be challenging for beginners because of rocks and other obstacles.
3.0 - 3.6 Not a casual float, but sailors with experience can manage.
4.1 - 4.5 Too strong for canoes, but depending on what they are, other vessels may go on.
4.6 - 5.0 High difficulty sailing, we do not suggest going unless your skills are confirmed.
