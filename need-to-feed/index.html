Why We Give

Renewing spirits and showing genuine love is our goal. Need to feed corp has given inspiration to people, seeking a way off of the streets. We have now surpassed our first year as a nonprofit organization and have obtained our 501c3.

“The number of homeless people in Dallas and Collin Counties have increased 9% over the past year." Dallas Morning News.

We are currently serving the homeless every 3rd Sunday of every month in Downtown Dallas and hope to obtain more of a permanent presence for the homeless. With your generous contributions, via monetary, time, etc... we will come closer to feeding, meeting the needs, and rehabilitating the homeless across our city, state, and eventually across our country!!

The Experience

From Streets To Sheets!

Meet Wilbur! He was one of our first participants at the Need to Feed events, and came to every event for help. Thankfully, Need to Feed was able to assist financially with obtaining a home, through the state, and deliver furniture, thanks to great supporters and givers like you!

Now, Wilbur attends the events as a volunteer to help serve the same people that are on the same streets as he used to be. On this page, we hope to share more success stories such as these, with your continued support!

Meet Mr. Melvin!

He was also one of our participants on the street and now Need to Feed Corp. is able to help him with food in his very own home!
